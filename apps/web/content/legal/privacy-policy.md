## 2. Information We Collect

We collect information to provide a better service to all our users. The types of personal information we collect include:

### A. Account Information

When you register for an account, we collect:

* Authentication credentials.

### B. Monitoring Configuration

* Performance metrics of your monitored endpoints.

## 4. Legal Basis for Processing

In compliance with Article 10 of Law No. 2014-038, we process your data based on:

1. **Contractual Necessity:** Processing is required to fulfill our terms of service.
2. **Legal Obligation:** Processing is required for tax, accounting, and regulatory compliance in Madagascar.
3. **Legitimate Interest:** To ensure the security and stability of our monitoring platform.

## 5. Data Storage and International Transfers

StatusFlow utilizes Cloudflare’s global edge network. While CodeCraft Consulting is based in Madagascar, your monitoring data may be processed in various geographical locations to ensure low-latency checks.

We ensure that any data transferred outside of Madagascar maintains a level of protection consistent with the requirements of the **Malagasy Data Protection Authority (CMDP)**.

## 6. Your Rights

Under Malagasy Law No. 2014-038, you have specific rights regarding your personal data:

* **Right of Access:** You may request details of the personal information we hold about you.
* **Right to Rectification:** You can request that we correct any inaccurate or incomplete information.
* **Right to Erasure:** You may request the deletion of your account and personal data, subject to legal retention requirements.
* **Right to Object:** You have the right to object to the processing of your data for direct marketing or legitimate reasons.

To exercise these rights, please contact us at **hello@codecraft.consulting**.
